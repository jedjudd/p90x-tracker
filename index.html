Your Phases · Level II

⚡ Phase I — Fat Shredder

High protein, low carb. Build lean muscle while burning fat. Your body becomes a fat-burning machine. This is the hardest phase — dig deep!

🥩 Protein ×7 🥛 Dairy ×3 🍎 Fruit ×1 🥦 Veg ×4 🥑 Fat ×1 🍚 Carb ×1 🍫 Snacks ×3 🧂 Cond ×2
2,400–2,999 cal/day · ~60% protein

🚀 Phase II — Energy Booster

Balanced macros. Add more carbs to fuel increasingly intense workouts. Your energy levels should noticeably improve in this phase.

🥩 Protein ×6 🥛 Dairy ×2 🍎 Fruit ×1 🥦 Veg ×3 🥑 Fat ×1 🍚 Carb ×3 🍫 Snacks ×3 🧂 Cond ×2
2,400–2,999 cal/day · ~40% protein, 40% carb

🏆 Phase III — Endurance Maximizer

Carb-heavy, lower protein. Fuel peak performance. Your body is now lean enough to maximize carbohydrates for elite-level endurance.

🥩 Protein ×3 🥛 Dairy ×1 🍎 Fruit ×3 🥦 Veg ×3 🥑 Fat ×1 🍚 Carb ×4 🍫 Snacks ×3 🧂 Cond ×3
2,400–2,999 cal/day · ~20% protein, 60% carb

Reduction Plan
Energy Without the Caffeine
These are the strategies that actually work for sustained, crash-free energy — especially for early morning workouts.
🎯 YOUR PERSONAL PLAN · 270mg → Goal
Step 1 (Weeks 1–4): Keep your 6 AM pre-workout (150mg). Cut the 10 AM dose from 120mg → 90mg → 60mg → 30mg → 0mg over 4–5 weeks. Replace it with a protein snack + cold water.

Step 2 (Weeks 5+): Once the 10 AM dose is gone, start weaning the pre-workout. Drop 25mg every 2 weeks: 150 → 125 → 100 → 75 → 50mg. At 50mg you're at green tea level — easy to maintain or continue cutting.

Eye relief: You should notice less eye strain within 2–3 weeks as your adrenal system stabilizes.
☀️ PRE-WORKOUT ALTERNATIVES
🍋 Cold Water + Electrolytes
Drink 16 oz cold water with a pinch of sea salt and squeeze of lemon immediately upon waking. Dehydration is the #1 cause of morning fatigue — this often works as well as pre-workout.
🍵 Green Tea (L-Theanine + Caffeine)
Matcha or strong green tea gives you ~70mg caffeine plus L-theanine, which smooths out the stimulant effect. No crash, no jitters. Many athletes prefer this over pre-workout for steady energy.
🧪 Rhodiola Rosea (adaptogen)
A natural adaptogen clinically shown to reduce fatigue and improve physical performance. Take 200–400mg 30 min before workouts. Zero caffeine — pairs well during your taper.
🕙 10AM ENERGY ALTERNATIVES
🥩 Protein Snack + 5-Minute Walk
Your 10 AM crash is often blood sugar, not caffeine deficiency. A Level II protein snack (Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, string cheese) + a 5-minute walk outside resets focus better than caffeine for most people.
💧 Hydration Check
By 10 AM post-workout you're likely mildly dehydrated. Try 20 oz water first and wait 10 minutes before reaching for caffeine. You may not need it at all.
👁️ Eye Fatigue Specifically
Tired eyes from screens respond to the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. Also consider blue-light glasses, increasing screen brightness, and making sure you're hitting your 8 glasses of water — dehydration directly causes eye strain.
